Help & Support

Getting Started

  1. Create an account by clicking "Login" and choosing "Sign Up"
  2. Select your account type (Parent, Teacher, or Student)
  3. Browse cards in the Shop section
  4. Customise and purchase cards using credits

Account Types

Student Accounts

  • Can create and customise cards
  • Receive credits from teachers
  • Save card drafts

Teacher Accounts

  • Access to Classroom Management
  • Can add/remove students
  • Distribute credits to students
  • Monitor student activity

Parent Accounts

  • Top up student credits
  • Create and purchase cards
  • Save card drafts

Creating Cards

  1. Choose a card template from the Shop
  2. Click "Personalise" to open the editor
  3. Add text using the text tools
  4. Drag and drop stickers
  5. Preview in 3D before purchasing
  6. Save as draft or complete purchase
Tip: Use the 3D preview to see how your card will look when printed or sent digitally.

Managing Credits

  • eCards cost 100 credits (£0.99)
  • Printable cards cost 200 credits (£1.99)
  • Teachers can distribute credits to students
  • Parents can top up student accounts
  • Check your balance in your account panel
Note: Credits cannot be transferred between accounts except through the teacher distribution system.

Classroom Management

  1. Access Classroom from your teacher account
  2. Add students using their email addresses
  3. Distribute credits to selected students
  4. Monitor student card creation activity
  5. Remove inactive students as needed
Important: Student accounts require parental consent for users under 16.

Troubleshooting

  • Clear your browser cache if images aren't loading
  • Ensure you're using a supported browser (Chrome 88+, Firefox 87+, Safari 14+, Edge 88+)
  • Check your internet connection for 3D preview issues
  • Contact support if credits aren't showing up correctly
