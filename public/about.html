About The Sustainables

Who We Are

The Sustainables is a not-for-profit organisation dedicated to providing free, high-quality educational resources to primary and secondary school teachers. Our mission is to promote accessibility to learning tools that empower students to make informed decisions about environmental sustainability and climate change.

Our Goal

We believe that every child should have the opportunity to engage with meaningful and impactful educational activities without financial barriers. Our organisation creates resources designed to inspire and educate, fostering a generation of environmentally conscious and responsible individuals.

Why This Platform?

To support our goal, we've developed this e-card platform to raise funds for maintaining and expanding our free educational tools. This aligns with our values by promoting sustainability while enabling teachers, students, and families to celebrate special moments in an eco-friendly way.
