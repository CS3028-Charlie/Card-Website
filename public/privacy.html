Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account information (email, username, role type)
  • Payment information (processed through PayPal)
  • Card designs and drafts
  • Classroom management data for teachers
  • Usage statistics and website activity

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used for:

  • Account management and authentication
  • Processing payments and managing credits
  • Enabling classroom management features
  • Saving and retrieving card designs
  • Improving our services and user experience

3. Student Data Protection

For students under 16:

  • Parental consent is required for account creation
  • Limited personal information is collected
  • No direct marketing communications are sent
  • Teachers act as data guardians within the classroom

4. Data Storage and Security

We protect your data through:

  • Secure SSL encryption for all data transfers
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited employee access to personal data
  • Secure cloud storage with regular backups
  • No storage of payment information on our servers

5. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies for:

  • Maintaining login sessions
  • Storing card design drafts
  • Remembering user preferences
  • Anonymous usage analytics

6. Third-Party Services

We work with these third-party services:

  • PayPal for payment processing
  • Google Analytics for website statistics
  • Cloud storage providers for data backup

7. Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request data deletion
  • Export your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time
